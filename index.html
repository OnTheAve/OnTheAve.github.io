Our Mission

Connecting Syracuse University to the food and culture that makes this city special.

When Syracuse students step onto campus, they often stick to the same familiar spots. But what about Iraqi cuisine? Turkish? Or just a spot with really affordable food that's off the beaten path?

On The Ave was created by Syracuse students who wanted more — more flavor, more culture, more connection. We built this app because we believe discovering local food should be a shared experience, not just a solo search.

Many underrecognized local businesses in Syracuse — particularly immigrant-owned restaurants and shops — are often overlooked despite offering some of the city's most unique, affordable, and meaningful cultural experiences.

On The Ave isn't just a food guide — it's a community. Share reviews, post photos, see what your friends are eating, and explore together. The more students use it, the stronger our local food scene becomes.

Our goal is twofold: give small businesses the spotlight they deserve, and bring students together through the food and culture that makes Syracuse special.
